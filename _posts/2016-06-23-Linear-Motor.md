The linear motor project was started after the failure of Pr8, which was in early 2017. After the failure of Pr8 I believed a portable railgun design that could meet my goal was impossible so I looked for alternatives, the linear motor being one of them. I felt that others had already reached the limits of the traditional coilgun design, and I believed by having a magnetic core in the driving solenoids and by placing them perpendicular to the motion of the projectile I could create a linear motor that could meet my needs.
