## Results
The point of the PR 6 was to test the basic design of a single turn augmented railgun. The inner rails where the armature makes contact were mad from aluminum and were 1/2in x 1/8in x 12in. The outer augmentation rails were made of aluminum and were 1in x 1/2in x 12 in. The power source was a 15000 uf 450 volt aluminum electrolytic capacitor. The armature was a 1/2 in. segment of 14 awg wire. 

The power supply was connected directly using a home made switch. There was a flash of light and a few sparks but the armature did not move.

I have come to the conclusion that the current was not great enough to produce a noticeable magnetic flux density and therfore the railgun produced an unnoticeable force.
