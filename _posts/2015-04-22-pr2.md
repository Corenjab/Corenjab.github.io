## Overview

<img src="/assets/images/Pr2.jpg" width="50%">

PR 2 consists of two parallel copper 14 awg wire which are taped to a neodymium bar magnet( 6 in. x  1/8 in. x  1/2). The bar magnet provides an augmenting magnetic field that is stronger than the field produced by the current through the wires which helps to produce a force strong enough to move a small projectile. Without the bar magnet the projectile simply welds to the two wires and does not move.

## Conclusion  
An augmenting magnetic field is necessary to produce a noticeable force. Without an augmentation field a railgun needs exorbitant amounts of current for minuscule force. More current means a larger power supply a larger power supply will not only cost a lot, but will also not be portable.
